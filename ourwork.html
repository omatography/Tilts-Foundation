OUR WORKS?

Yes! we are a Non Governmental Organisation. We started with the work of providing internet and mobiles for students in Najni village of Nandurbar district of Maharastra. It was a huge success which made us a recognition in Maharastra. Then we moved further to Madhya Pradesh, Chattisgarh and parts of Bihar. As we expanded, donators and volunteers began to increase.

We like to thank our donors and other people who made this initiative a great success.

WHAT'S NEXT?

Next we are planning to take free online classes for the students. Our volunteer team is preparing well for ths. The classes include portions covered as same as in schools in a very creative manner. In this way the students could learn very easily in their understandable language.

This program will start as early as possible. Our tutors team is tremendously working on it.
