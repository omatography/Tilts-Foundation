ABOUT US

In this pandemic period, every student is dependant on online classes. Also it needs various resources like mobile phones, Laptops, internet connection etc. We are blessed to have these all resources but not all are. In some rural areas there are people who dosen't have access to smartphones and stable internet. In some areas the have access but cannot afford these things.

Our organisation helps these students by making them available these resources. In this pandemic time, we don't want them to be helpless, thus started TILTS FOUNDATION.

THE COVID-19 EFFECT

Presently whole world is facing the COVID-19 pandemic. All are facing economic crisis, schools, colleges etc are closed and everyone are at home. We students are having lot of resources to study, enjoy and pass the time, Netflix and chill etc. There are many such students who are unable to study and no access to internet.

These students must be taken to consideration. Our education should not be stopped dut to this pandemic.
