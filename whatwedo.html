HOW THIS STARTED?

The word TILTS derives from a latvian word which means bridge. We want to create a bridge between the two divisions of the society. One side where every resources are available and comfortable for education, other one is of poverty, scarcity and non-accesibiity of resources. Thus make the poor students pass easily to the other side they need and they deserve.

Thus the name of our foundation created as TILTS FOUNDATION. And we are really proud that our foundation is growing day by day.

Charity sees the need, not the cause
German Proverb
WHAT WE DO?

We provide poor students access to the resources like Smartphones, Laptops, Stable Net connection and other needed resources so that they could afford as well as access it. Online education is the solution to keep the education life cycle running in the pandemic situation. We ensure those needed ones will get access and could afford online education.

We also adopt certain govt schools in rural areas to get them access to online education system and make it available to them.

In Charity there is no excess.
Francis Bacon
WHAT INSPIRED US?

There was a news on july 18, 2020 that a Rajasthani Boy Climbs A Mountain Daily To Catch Network To Attend Online Classes.He is a student of Jawahar Navodaya Vidhyalay from a small village Darura in Barmer district. He started to climb a mountain everyday to find network for internet so he could attend online classes. For detailed news Click Here

Hats off from the TILTS Foundation for this immense dedication.

This inspired us to think that students are not getting enough access to online education and this inspired us to start this Foundation.
